# 🚓 Metro Police – Smart Digital Policing Platform

Metro Police is an AI-enabled digital policing system designed to modernize law enforcement services in metropolitan areas. The platform connects citizens, police officers, and administrators through a centralized online system to ensure faster response, transparency, and smarter crime management.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Traditional police systems rely heavily on paperwork and manual processes, resulting in slow service and limited transparency. **Metro Police** replaces these outdated methods with a secure, digital, and intelligent platform that supports online reporting, real-time tracking, and data-driven policing.

---

## 🎯 Motivation

- Manual police reporting is slow and inefficient  
- Citizens face difficulty tracking case progress  
- Lack of smart crime monitoring and prediction tools  
- No centralized, mobile-friendly policing system  
- Need for transparency and faster police response  

---

## 🧠 Project Objective

To transform traditional policing into a **modern, transparent, and AI-powered digital system** that improves citizen access, accelerates police response, and supports intelligent crime monitoring in metropolitan cities.

---

## 👥 User Roles

- **Citizen**
- **Police**
- **Admin (System Administrator / Government Supervisor)**

---

## 🧑‍✈️ Police Features

- Case Management & Status Updates  
- Missing Person & Missing Vehicle Auto-Match System  
- Criminal Database Search  
- FIR / Case Entry  
- Smart Police Patrolling Heatmap  

### Details
- Assign and update cases submitted by citizens  
- Auto-match missing reports with CCTV and police databases  
- Search criminal records using face, nickname, vehicle, or pattern  
- Direct FIR submission with evidence and suspect information  
- Visualize crime hotspots for optimized patrol routes  

---

## 🧍 Citizen Features

- Online GD / Crime Reporting  
- Traffic Fine Check & Online Payment  
- Lost Item Report & Certificate  
- Police Clearance Certificate  
- Crime Risk Map  
- “Safe Route” GPS for Night Travel  

### Details
- Submit complaints with photos/videos and receive a GD number  
- View and pay traffic fines digitally  
- Download official lost-item certificates  
- Apply for police clearance online  
- View safe/unsafe zones using historical crime data  
- Get safest route suggestions using crime and patrol data
